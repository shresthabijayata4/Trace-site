ABOUT US

Training and Consultancy Center for Employment and Enterprising (TRACE) Pvt. Ltd established in 2008 under the company registration act of Nepal and has been actively involving in providing the skills training for unemployed youth of Nepal and link them in employment since its establishment. Up to now TRACE has supported around 10,000 people for their training and 95% of them are at employment. It has extended its services more than 18 districts. Apart from the skills training, the main expertise of TRACE are labor market assessment, training modeling, technical assistance for the development of curricula and manuals, conduction of ‘Training of Trainers (TOT)’ as well as post-training support to the individual graduates like coaching, counseling, consultancy, facilitation for the financial services for the gainful employment or the entrepreneurship. TRACE has developed 6 training manuals (Mason, Plasterer, Constriction Carpentry, Furniture Maker, Dress Maker and Khoto Collators) with the support of CTEVT. Moreover it has strong work experience on business skills training (Business motivation, Business plan development) with the use of innovative ideas. TRACE has also a good experience of modeling and conducting the Trainings for Trainers and has conducted Training of Trainers (TOT) for 200 internal as well as external instructors and trainers. The TRACE has sufficient human resources to carry out their activity which includes 24 regular staffs, 256 skillful trainers in more than 24 trades and occupations.


Recently, the TRACE with its own initiation had accomplished a relief support to Earth Quake (EQ) affected households (1000 HHs) of Sindhupalchok District with a composite package of food, clothes and tarpaulins. Moreover, it has also initiated re-construction related initiatives in Rasuwa and Gorkha Districts with the support of ADRA Japan, CORDAID/Paribartan, OM Nepal World Jewish Relief and CSRC. HELVETAS Swiss Intercooperation Nepal, its continuous donor, has also partnering TRACE for the implementation of its ‘Skills for Reconstruction/EF-SR’ program which is anticipated to be ended in January 2018.



Objectives of the organization


  • Provide skill training as per labour market demand and provide coaching, counselling and consultancy (CCC) services for self-employment going youths.
  • Provide entrepreneurial training, coaching, counselling and consultancy to the unemployed youths and micro entrepreneurs as per market demand.
  • Provide training to produce market demanded products, support to marketing and sales of the products.
  • Provide enterprising and employment creation services as demanded by companies I/NGOs, and GOs.
