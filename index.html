TRACE

August 7, 2008

Training and Consultancy Center for Employment and Enterprising (TRACE) Pvt. Ltd established in 2008.

To provide skills training for unemployed youth of Nepal and link them in employment. Since our establishment 95% are employed through our training. Conducted more than 200+ training helping almost 10000 peoples. There are important clients helping us through our journey. Trace helped in the past earthquake by donating funds in Sindupalchowk.
